Software Engineering is the branch of computer science that deals with the design, development, testing, and maintenance of software. It applies engineering principles to create reliable, efficient, and high-quality computer programs that solve real-world problems.

Unlike basic programming, software engineering focuses on planning and managing the entire process of building software  from understanding what users need to delivering and maintaining the final product. It follows a structured approach known as the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), which includes stages like analysis, design, coding, testing, and maintenance.

Software engineers work in many areas such as web development, mobile apps, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. Their work helps power the technology we use every day  from phones and computers to cars, hospitals etc.
